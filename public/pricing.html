FAQ

Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We use Supabase for secure authentication and your tasks are private — only you can see them.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards through Stripe — Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

Do I need a credit card for the free plan?

No credit card required for the free plan. Sign up with just your email and start using TaskFlow immediately.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Yes, you can upgrade to Pro or go back to Free at any time from your account settings.

Is there a student discount?

We offer a 50% student discount. Contact us with your school email to get your discount code.
